## Demo Notes

- The preview assets use the existing brand palette: green tea, cream, warm gold, blush, and mint.
- Screenshots were captured from the running Vite application to avoid mock-only presentation.
- The OpenGraph image is wired through the static HTML metadata, runtime SEO helper, PWA manifest, and service worker app shell.
- No demo asset changes alter routing, stores, shopping behavior, theme switching, or localization.
